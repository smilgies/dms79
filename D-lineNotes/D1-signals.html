D1 Signals Viewer


Operations staff only : Monitor mono vacuum and other signals during line-up (Peter Revesz)
(On analysis PC.)
  • check box "connected - all signals appear
  • doubleclick D1 mono vacuum signals to select:
    • DC_CC_SLT_US_T : upstream slits (white beam)
    • DC_CC_COF_T: coffin
    • DC_CC_BV_DS_T: beam viewer downstream
      • cold cathode gauge signals will appear in middle panel "selected"
  • click each selected signal in middle panel: it will be highlighted
  • click ">>":
    • realtime plots of selected signals appear
    •  ">>" changes to "||"
      • click "||" to pause acquisition (e.g. for adding other signals)
