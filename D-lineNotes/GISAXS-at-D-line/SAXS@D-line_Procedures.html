SAXS @ D-line: Procedures

Switch to CCD mode
  • move beamstop into the beam > mv bsx 0; mv bsz 0;  wbs
  • verify that beamstop is in the beam : Idet counts should be small
  • close shutter  >  shclose
  • check: Imon counts should be small
  • enable ccd macros  >  ccdon
  • go into hutch and remove ion chamber
  • move CCD flush against marker clamp trying to avoid angular offset
  • interlock hutch, enable and open garage door
  • take a test image for 0.1 sec  >  corr 1 0.1
  • if necessary, finetune beamstop position
    • move bsx or bsz in steps of 0.1 mm and take test image > corr 1 0.1
Take direct beam image (if necessary)
  • go into hutch and attach absorber (lead tape @ 10 keV) to entrance of flight path
  • interlock hutch and open garage door
  • move beam stop out of the way > mv bsx 2
  • TAKE TEST IMAGE > corr 1 0.1
  • if image okay (all dark), try 1 sec and then 10sec exposure
  • move beamstop back in > mv bsx 0
  • go into hutch and remove absorber
Switch to Line-up Mode
  • disable garage door (switch on controller)
  • disable ccd macros  >  ccdoff
  • go into hutch, move CCD back, put ion chamber in and change samples
  • interlock hutch and open CCD shutter  >  shopen
  • new data file  >  newfile [sample name]
  • direct beam line-up
    • move beamstop out > wbs; mv bsx 2
    • move sample to ZERO position > wsam; mv sx 0
    • scan sample through beam > dscan samx -19 19 76 1
    • note sx positions with promising absorption to take images there
  • beamstop line-up (if needed)
    • > dscan bsx -3 3 30 1
    • move bsx to the center of the blocked area using the numerical scan output
    • > dscan bsz -3 3 30 1
    • move bsz to the center of the blocked area using the numerical scan output
( note: after  ">"  follows the spec command, as it is typed in )
